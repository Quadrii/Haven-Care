about us

The New Haven Care Recruitment is a leading independent recruitment company with years of experience and a reputation for providing quality positions for staff in the social care, industrial, commercial, hospitality, blitz cleaning, local authority and public sectors.
Numerous companies and organisations rely on our expert team to fulfil their staffing requirements and our jobs board regularly features positions in a wide range of sectors. We are in admiration of the skill and dedication of our care workforce, all of whom have care industry expertise. As well as temporary and permanent positions, the other services and resources we provide includes:

  • CV Service
  • DBS Checks
  • Training and Support
  • Appraisals

Register with us online before coming in for a friendly interview followed by a short skills tests. Our test allows us to identify your strengths and weaknesses which in turn assists us in identifying jobs that suit your skills and training that addresses your weaknesses. We have a diverse selection of permanent, temporary and contractual posts available, so why not browse the jobs available at the moment and see if there are any vacancies suitable for you.


If you are a candidate searching for a specific role and want to utilise the services of a trusted, professional recruitment agency contact us at New Haven Care Recruitment today. Call: +01 708553112 or click here to send us an email enquiry.
